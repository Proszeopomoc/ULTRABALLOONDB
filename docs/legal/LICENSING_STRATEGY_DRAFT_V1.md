# UltraBalloonDB Licensing Strategy Draft V1

Status: non-binding draft. This file is not a license grant.

## Candidate model

- core database engine and unique execution/trust modules: BSL 1.1 candidate;
- SDKs, public protocol definitions, headers and examples: Apache-2.0 candidate;
- enterprise features and support: separate commercial agreement;
- trademarks: separate trademark policy;
- private provenance/fingerprint material: outside the public repository.

## Intended outcome

Individuals, researchers and companies should be able to evaluate, test and
integrate UltraBalloonDB with low friction. Production, OEM, managed-service or
enterprise use may require a commercial agreement according to a legally
reviewed Additional Use Grant.

## Current restriction

No final license is selected by this architecture gate. No existing repository
license is replaced. Final wording, contribution policy, CLA/DCO, warranty
disclaimer and commercial terms require legal review.

## Pre-1.0 product status

Until explicitly promoted, UltraBalloonDB should be presented as experimental
or pre-production software without SLA or production warranty. Users remain
responsible for backups, validation and risk assessment, subject to applicable
law and the final license.
